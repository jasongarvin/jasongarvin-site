The World as Inspiration

When I'm not designing websites or retail space, and I'm not buried in operational work for Total Escape Games, I like to explore my local surroundings and take pictures of anything that might inspire me in the future.

Even off the web, user experience and design are everywhere. I try to learn from the best practices of construction, architecture, even natural beauty to understand how best to create new projects or update existing ones based on the incredible material around me.
