Total Escape Games

About

I've been with Total Escape Games for 6 years, and during that time have made great strides not only in growing the business, but also in rocketing us into the modern era of retail, events, and community management.

What I Do

My key focuses with Total Escape Games these days are twofold: setting up clear procedures and scaffolding for staff so they can do their best work, and revising our entire digital presence.

Over the course of my time with the company, sales have increased a great deal and we've truly come into our own as a stable, inclusive local game store doing a high volume of sales while maintaining our small business atmosphere.

Fill and Fire

I'm also at Fill and Fire, and that's a thing. I've learned a lot.

What I've Learned

Jason is a small business professional with a focus on operations management, who in recent years has begun to lean further into software in both an attempt to expand his skillset and also to grow Total Escape Games into the modern era of retail, events, and digital commerce.

While Jason has a variety of skills, he most specializes in designing and implementing frameworks and operational scaffolding to ensure and support the success of his team. He has also picked up a number of design and user-experience related skills during his time with Fill and Fire, a small company devoted to solving the events preregistration space.
