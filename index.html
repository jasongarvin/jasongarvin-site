Total Escape Games

About

I've been with Total Escape Games for almost 7 years, and during that time have made great strides not only in growing the business, but also in rocketing us into the modern era of retail, events, and community growth.

What I Do

My key focuses with Total Escape Games these days are twofold: setting up clear procedures and scaffolding for staff so they can do their best work, and revising our entire digital presence.

Over the course of my time with the company, sales have increased a great deal and we've truly come into our own as a stable, inclusive local game store doing a high volume of sales while maintaining our small business atmosphere.

Fill and Fire

I'm also affiliated with Fill and Fire, where I've learned a great deal about working in a small startup environment as well as user experience design and project management.

What I do

With Fill and Fire I focus most on establishing a solid business foundation to support the team in achieving our goals. This most includes building out clear business and branding plans and setting up clear paths to achieving our ambitions in solving the event pre-registration landscape.

I've done a great deal of tasks for Fill and Fire, and while I'm leveraging my skills now towards business development, I also learned a great deal about user research, testing, and UI/UX design. From my time with the team I've grown to understand how incredibly important it is to be in your consumer's shoes and clearly understand their needs.
