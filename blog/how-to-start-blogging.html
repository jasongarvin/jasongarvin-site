Overcoming Inertia:
How to Start Blogging

Jason Garvin 07/23/2023

What better way to overcome my own inertia about blogging than to help teach you how to get rolling in your own life, too. It's a sort of meta-blog, if you will. I've also been writing on Threads since it came out, which is also a sort of Meta blog, you know?

Anyway, let's learn how to get started with a new idea. Maybe it's a blog, like in my case, or maybe it's a new product you want to develop, a book you want to write, a hike you want to take, or anything else. It can be big, but it's probably best for now to give yourself a break with something small and easily-achieved

Frankly, the best way to start something new is to just do it. Which, if you're like me, is totally unhelpful advice. So let's unpack that!
