Me, Summarized

Total Escape Games

About

Total Escape Games is a local game store located in Broomfield, CO that's been operating for 14+ years. In addition to a varied retail selection of card games, board games, miniatures, paints & supplies, toys, and more, TEG is known for running excellent events for all varieties of games and systems.

Jason is coming up on 7 years managing Total Escape Games this year, during which time he's held a variety of roles. Some highlights include event management and coordination, digital content creation, operations management, business development, and brand design.

Jason at TEG

Over the course of his time with the company, sales have increased substantially and TEG has truly come into its own as a stable, inclusive local game store doing a high volume of sales while maintaining the same, familiar small business atmosphere.
