About Me What I've done & my journey

Me, Summarized

Mainly I've spent my life helping small businesses, especially local game stores, to succeed in their local environment. I also consult with software companies to build tech solutions for the gaming industry, and build websites for peers and businesses on the side.

I have a variety of skills, but am most specialized in designing and implementing frameworks and operational scaffolding to ensure and support the success of my teams.

I've especially enjoyed learning front-end web development, but have experience in Python (mostly data science and scripting), user experience design, UI layout design, and project management.

Fill and Fire

I've also been with Fill and Fire coming up on 4 years now, where I went from consulting to offer a perspective as a game store to managing our product development and helping design our user experience.

My Efforts with Fill and Fire

Fill and Fire is still in its youth, and we've yet to fully release our product or finish our first phase of development. Life moves slow as we work on the project aside our primary jobs, but as a team we've grown tremendously at project management and feature design.

So far, I've queued up a great deal of redesign work with user experience in mind, ranging from UI overhauls to a complete rework of how our accounts function and how stores interface with event creation. I do design research, explore our ideal customers' needs and wants, and try to design a system that pre-empts their needs and delivers and excellent event-going experience.

Growing into UX

My time with Fill and Fire has given me invaluable experience in UX design, which I've found a deep passion for doing. I can't wait to continue growing as a designer and professional, even as I wear a variety of other hats to help balance our team.
