About Me

A story about taking life's side paths
And what they represent

My mission

Everything I do is based around the belief that in a post-AI world, the human experience is what's left. When machines can automate and improve our lives in myriad ways, the remaining element that keeps us connected with one another is experience

My dream is a world where people can seek mutual experiences, belong to a community, and where everything we do and see is tailor-fit to our unique needs

The beginning of my journey

I got my Bachelor's of Psychology from Colorado State University in pursuit of understanding people. Now, many years later, I'm putting that knowledge to work handling operations and digital content creation for Total Escape Games and product design for Fill and Fire

My projects and skills

I excel in business strategy, operations, and growth. To supplement that and reach the next level, I've been studying marketing, design, and UX

I've been digging deep into web design & development for the past two years in pursuit of a more modern game industry and better digital parity between the in-person and digital experience at Total Escape Games

At Fill and Fire, I use my web development and UX experience to create and deliver our MVP product, conduct usability testing, and evaluate demand. The goal is to tie smooth preregistration into game stores to provide a better event experience for players and stores alike

You can see what I'm working on by visiting my GitHub profile including the source code for this website
