About Me

A journey of connection and innovation

My mission

In a world increasingly driven by automation and artificial intelligence, I've embarked on a journey to rediscover the power of human connection

My mission is to create experiences that foster meaningful interactions, cultivate a sense of community, and tailor our digital world to real people's goals

AI and robotics are getting closer and closer each day to automating our basic needs. What remains, then, is our day-to-day experience and the interfaces through which we see the world

The beginning of my journey

My passion for understanding human behavior led me to pursue a Bachelor's of Psychology from Colorado State University

Armed with this knowledge, I've spent the last decade delving into the realms of operations, digital content creation, and product design, driven by a desire to bridge the gap between technology and the human experience

My projects and skills

I'm constantly taking on new responsibilities to push the boundaries of what I know and what I'm capable of achieving

At Total Escape Games, I'm build operations, strategy, and run our digital content engine, meaning I'm constantly exploring ways to improve our in-store experience and extend its magic to the digital world

In my pursuit of a modernized game industry, I've dedicated the past four years to learning software development and web design. My goal is to build seamless digital experiences that complement the in-person thrills of Total Escape Games

With Fill and Fire, I'm focused on revolutionizing event pre-registration to reduce the pain many of us who run events experience every day. Leveraging my expertise in UX, I'm working to make pre-registration an empowering and helpful experience, rather than a necessary evil

My journey is a testament to the power of continuous learning. Only by explorating our own potential do we discover our true passions, develop unique skill sets, and grow to create a meaningful impact on the world around us

I invite you to join me on this journey. Together, let's explore the intersection of human connection and technological innovation, creating a world where every interaction is an experience worth cherishing

To learn more about my work, you can visit my GitHub profile which includes the source code for this website, The Total Escape Games home page to see my business, or my Threads account where I ocassionally post insight into my life and thinking
