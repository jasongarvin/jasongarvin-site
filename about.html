About Me

Crafting meaningful experiences in business and on the web

In our digital age, I believe forging genuine human connections should be the driving force behind exceptional brand design.

As a business owner and product designer, my goal is to blend emerging technology with genuine human interaction to create amazing experiences.

My History

Motivated by a curiosity for human behavior, I earned my degree in Psychology from Colorado State University. This foundation has led me on a 7+ year journey of merging psychological principles with operational strategy, digital content creation, and user experience design.

My diverse skillset includes business strategy, process optimization, business growth, UX/UI design, and web development.

My Skills

I've learned to have a diverse skillset as a small business owner. Here are some highlights:

  • Business strategy & operations
  • Small business growth
  • User Experience design
  • Web development
  • Creativity & innovation

My Vision

As technology continues to advance and its influence grows, I want to bring back the power of real human connection. My dream is to create experiences that bring people together, build communities, and make digital spaces feel personal and relatable.

The way we use these new technologies and tools will shape our future. How we spend our daily lives and interact with the digital world is paramount.

I'm committed to designing these touchpoints with creativity, empathy, and an understanding of human psychology - ensuring every interaction leaves a lasting, positive impact.
