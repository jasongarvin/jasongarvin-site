About Me What I do & how I got here

Me, Summarized

I help small business, especially local game stores, to succeed and consult with software companies in our industry from time to time. I've especially enjoyed learning front-end web development, but have experience in Python (mostly data science and scripting), user experience design, UI layout design, and project management.

I have a variety of skills, but am most specialized in designing and implementing frameworks and operational scaffolding to ensure and support the success of my teams. I've also picked up a number of design and user-experience related skills during my time with Fill and Fire, a small company devoted to solving the event pre-registration space.
