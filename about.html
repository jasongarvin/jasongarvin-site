Total Escape Games

About

Jason is a small business professional with a focus on operations management, who in recent years has begun to lean further into software in both an attempt to expand his skillset and also to grow Total Escape Games into the modern era of retail, events, and digital commerce.

While Jason has a variety of skills, he most specializes in designing and implementing frameworks and operational scaffolding to ensure and support the success of his team. He has also picked up a number of design and user-experience related skills during his time with Fill and Fire, a small company devoted to solving the events preregistration space.
