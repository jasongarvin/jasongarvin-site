About Me

What I've done and where I'm going

I create experience-driven business operations at a startup and small-business scale to bridge the divide between businesses and consumers

The beginning of my journey

I got my Bachelor's of Psychology from Colorado State University in pursuit of understanding people. Now, many years later, I'm putting that knowledge to work handling operations and digital content creation for Total Escape Games and product design for Fill and Fire

My mission

Everything I do is based around the belief that in a post-AI world, the human experience is what's left. When machines can automate and improve our lives in myriad ways, the remaining element that keeps us connected with one another is experience

My dream is a world where people can seek mutual experiences, be a part of community, and where everything we do and see is tailor-fit to us and our needs

My skills and projects

First and foremost I excel in business, operations, and digital content strategy. I'm heavily underway in modernizing Total Escape Games, and the hobby industry overall, to bring our expertise to a digital future

To achieve the dream of digital parity between the in-store and online experience, I've been digging deep into software (and web) design for the past two years. Our website, social media, and Discord server are all maintained solely by me, with more improvements on the way

I additionally leverage my web development and UX design experience for Fill and Fire, currently at MVP, to conduct usability testing and evaluate market fit. We have a great deal of design and UX changes queued up for the next phase, and have been thrilled with the experiences we've created for event-goers and stores alike

You can see what I've got cooking by visiting my GitHub profile and can even view the source code for this website
